Project note: Heat planning data integration pilot, Landkreis Beispiel.

We tested linking the building registry (ALK/ALKIS) with energy consumption data from the local utility. The main challenge was address matching: roughly 15% of utility records could not be matched to cadastral parcels due to formatting differences in street names and house numbers.

A manual reconciliation pass resolved about half of the mismatches. The remaining 7–8% represent genuine data gaps (buildings without utility connections, or utility records for demolished structures). These gaps need to be flagged in any heat demand assessment rather than silently excluded.
