From a conversation with the Bürgermeister of a mid-sized municipality.

The mayor expressed concern that the heat planning mandate arrives without adequate staffing support. "We are expected to plan the energy transition for 35,000 residents with two people in our building department." He noted that neighboring municipalities are pooling resources through their Landkreis, but coordination adds its own overhead.

He was supportive of the policy goal but skeptical about the timeline. His main request was for standardized planning tools that reduce the need for specialized consultants.
