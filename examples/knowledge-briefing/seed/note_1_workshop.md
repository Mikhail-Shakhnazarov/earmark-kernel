Notes from the regional heat planning workshop, March 2026.

Several municipalities reported that they lack in-house capacity to assess building stock
for heat planning purposes. Smaller municipalities (under 20,000 residents) typically have
no dedicated energy planning staff. Workshop participants from three Landkreise confirmed
that building age data is often incomplete or held in incompatible cadastral systems.

The consensus recommendation was to develop shared assessment templates that could be
adapted locally rather than requiring each municipality to build from scratch.
