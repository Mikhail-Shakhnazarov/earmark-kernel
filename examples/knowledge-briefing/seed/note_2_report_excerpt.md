Excerpt from Federal Heat Planning Progress Report, Q1 2026.

As of January 2026, approximately 14% of German municipalities with populations above
10,000 have completed a formal communal heat plan (kommunale Wärmeplanung). The federal
target requires completion for all municipalities above 10,000 by mid-2028. Current
completion rates suggest that roughly 60% of affected municipalities will need to begin
planning processes within the next 12 months to meet the deadline.

Funding through the National Climate Initiative (NKI) covers up to 90% of planning costs
for eligible municipalities, but uptake has been uneven across Bundesländer.
